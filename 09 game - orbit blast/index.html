Game Instructions

  • Press A and D to rotate player left and right.
  • Press key W to fire player to enemy.
  • In mobile device you can swipe Left and Right to rotate player left and right. And Top screen to fire player.
  • On every enemy hit you got one point and earn one life point.
  • On every miss fire you lose one life point.
  • If you lost all life points the game will be over.
