Game Instructions

  • Press A and D to rotate the player left and right.
  • Press key W to fire player to enemy.
  • On mobile devices, swipe Left and Right to rotate player left and right. And Tap screen to fire player at the enemy.
  • Each successful hit on an enemy earns you one point and an extra life.
  • Every missed shot costs you one life point.
  • The game ends when you lose all your life points.
